• Rules__
Basic models of flocking behaviour are controlled by three simple rules:__

• Separation__
Avoid crowding neighbours (short range repulsion)__
• Alignment__
Steer towards average heading of neighbours__
• Cohesion__
Steer towards average position of neighbours (long range attraction)__
• With these three simple rules, the flock moves in an extremely realistic way, creating complex motion and interaction that would be extremely hard to create otherwise.
